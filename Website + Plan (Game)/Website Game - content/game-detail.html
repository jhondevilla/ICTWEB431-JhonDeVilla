DETAILS OF ROLES

In the dynamic landscape of Overwatch 2, the character roster is ingeniously categorized into three distinct roles, each playing a pivotal part in shaping the ebb and flow of the game. The stalwart Tanks, such as Reinhardt and Orisa, lead the charge, their robust armor absorbing damage and creating crucial space for the team. Damage heroes, epitomized by the agile Tracer and precision-focused Widowmaker, are the relentless assailants, dealing significant harm and securing eliminations to tip the scales in their favor. The Support category, featuring the likes of Mercy and Lucio, ensures the team's survival by providing healing, utility, and strategic advantages. As Overwatch 2 evolves, so do these roles, with the promise of new heroes bringing innovative playstyles and synergies to the mix. The careful balance and collaboration among Tanks, Damage, and Support heroes are essential, creating a dynamic and ever-changing battlefield where adapting strategies and embracing teamwork are the keys to victory.
