GAME DETAILS

Overwatch 2 is the highly anticipated sequel to the original Overwatch, a team-based first-person shooter. Overwatch 2 aimed to build upon the success of its predecessor with several notable additions and improvements:

  • New Heroes and Maps: Overwatch 2 was expected to introduce new playable heroes and maps.
  • PvE (Player versus Environment) Mode: One of the significant features of Overwatch 2 was the introduction of a PvE mode. Unlike the original game, which primarily focused on PvP (Player versus Player) gameplay.
  • Hero Changes and Role Updates: The game was expected to bring changes to existing heroes, possibly introducing new abilities or tweaking existing ones. The introduction of role passives and adjustments to the tank role were some of the reported changes.

ROLES

  • Tanks are front-line heroes that excel at absorbing damage, creating space, and initiating engagements. They are typically sturdy and have high health pools.
  • Damage heroes are designed to deal significant damage to enemies. They often have varying levels of mobility and can be crucial in securing eliminations.
  • Support heroes are dedicated to healing and providing utility to their teammates. They play a crucial role in sustaining the team's health and enabling offensive and defensive strategies.
